About Me

Welcome

I'm Fia, a bead stringer, making people feel whole in the fashion world is my business priority, by being creative with accessories to satisfy my customers and making them contact my brand again for more service. I'm also a writer and a freelancer, I studied English Literature at the University Of Ilorin. I love to explore my creativity when there is an opportunity. I love to dance, write and make beads.
